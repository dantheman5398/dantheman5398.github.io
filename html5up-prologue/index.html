About Me

My name is Steven Roberts and I have worked as an Entrepreneur and Landscaper for 35 years. My crew and I primarily work in Latham, Loudonville, and Menands but I am willing to go a bit further if you want a big job done. I have an Associates Degree in Horticulture and a Bachelors in Botany. Additionally I began working on a Masters in Landscape Architecture before realizing I prefered running my own buisness with just one goal: to make my customers happy. Whether its a simple mowing or an enormous, creative job I am happy to meet with you for a free estimate to make your vision become a reality.
